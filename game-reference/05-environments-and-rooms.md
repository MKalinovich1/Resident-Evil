# Resident Evil (1996) — Environments & Room Reference

## Overview of Game Areas

The game takes place across four main areas, each with a distinct visual identity and atmosphere:

```
1. Spencer Mansion (1F, 2F, Basement)
2. Courtyard & Garden
3. Guardhouse / Residence
4. Underground Caves & Laboratory
```

---

## Area 1: Spencer Mansion

The heart of the game. A sprawling Victorian-era mansion built by Ozwell E. Spencer, secretly designed with hidden passages, traps, and an underground lab by architect George Trevor.

### Visual Style
- Gothic Victorian architecture
- Dark wood paneling, ornate wallpaper, chandeliers
- Marble floors, red carpets, grandfather clocks
- Moonlight streaming through windows
- Oil paintings, armor suits, trophy heads on walls
- Creaky floorboards, dust particles in light shafts

## Area 2: Courtyard & Garden

### Visual Style
- Overgrown garden paths, crumbling stone walls
- Iron gates, stone fountains, hedgerows
- Night sky visible, moonlight, fog
- Stone pathways with moss and cracks
- Connecting area between mansion and guardhouse

### Key Locations

| Location | Description | Key Events / Items | Enemies |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Garden Path** | Stone pathway through overgrown gardens | Connect mansion exits to guardhouse | Cerberus, Crows, Adders |
| **Crest Gate (Gate of New Life)** | Large stone gate requiring 4 crests (Sun, Moon, Star, Wind) | Major progression gate | None |

| **Shed / Tool Room** | Small garden outbuilding | Tools, crank, supplies | None |

## Area 3: Guardhouse / Residence

A separate building connected to the mansion by underground tunnels. Originally a dormitory for Umbrella researchers.

### Visual Style
- More modern/industrial than the mansion
- Concrete and metal mixed with wood paneling
- Fluorescent lighting (some flickering/broken)
- Water damage everywhere — pipes, leaks, flooded areas
- Overgrown with plant tendrils from Plant 42
- Creepy, claustrophobic corridors

### Key Locations

| Location | Description | Key Events / Items | Enemies |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Entrance Hall** | Small lobby of the guardhouse | Starting point, item box | Zombies |
| **Dormitory Rooms** | Individual researcher bedrooms | Files, documents, supplies | Zombies |
| **Aqua Ring (Flooded Room)** | Large room flooded with water, glass tanks | **Neptune** (mutant sharks) lurk in the water. Must drain the room. | Neptune |
| **Aqua Ring Control Room** | Room with pump controls | Drain the Aqua Ring water | None |
| **Chemical Lab** | Laboratory with mixing equipment | **V-JOLT creation** — mix chemicals to weaken Plant 42 | None |
| **Plant 42 Room** | Top floor room completely overtaken by Plant 42 | **Plant 42 boss fight** | Plant 42 (boss), Wasps |
| **Gallery / Recreation Room** | Common area for researchers | Files, documents | Web Spinners |
| **Basement Tunnels** | Dark connecting tunnels beneath guardhouse | Route to caves | Web Spinners, Small Spiders |

## Area 4: Underground Caves & Laboratory

The deepest areas of the game — the true heart of Umbrella's operations.

### Underground Caves

#### Visual Style
- Natural rocky cave formations
- Dripping water, stalactites, narrow passages
- Dim lighting — flashlight/torch needed
- Spider webs covering walls
- Boulder traps on slopes

| Location | Description | Key Events / Items | Enemies |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Cave Entrance** | Transition from mansion/graveyard to caves | — | Web Spinners |
| **Spider Nest** | Large cave chamber filled with webs | **Black Tiger boss fight** | Black Tiger (boss), Small Spiders |
| **Boulder Corridor** | Sloped tunnel with rolling boulder trap | Must dodge rolling boulders (Indiana Jones-style) | Environmental hazard |
| **Enrico's Cave** | Open cave area where Enrico is found | Enrico's death scene — shot mid-warning | None (cutscene) |
| **Underground Stream** | Waterway through caves | Connecting path | Adders |

### Underground Laboratory

#### Visual Style
- Stark contrast to the mansion — sterile, modern, high-tech
- White/grey walls, fluorescent lights, glass partitions
- Computer terminals, containment tubes, operating tables
- Bio-hazard warnings, emergency lighting in some areas
- Gradually more chaotic deeper in — signs of the outbreak
- Some areas damaged/destroyed with exposed pipes and wires

| Location | Description | Key Events / Items | Enemies |
|---------|-------------|-------------------|---------|
| **Lab Entrance / Elevator** | Elevator descent from caves into lab | Transition moment — "it gets real" | None |
| **Lab Corridors** | Clean white hallways with doors | MO Disk card readers to unlock doors | Chimeras, Hunters |
| **Visual Data Room** | Computer room with large monitors | MO Disk puzzles, lab passcodes | Chimeras |
| **Power Room** | Generator/power core room | Power restoration puzzle | Chimeras |
| **Research Lab** | Main research area with equipment and experiment notes | Documents revealing T-Virus origins | Chimeras |
| **Prison Cells** | Holding cells for human test subjects | Disturbing notes from prisoners | Zombies (special) |
| **Tyrant Containment Room** | Large room with a fluid-filled tank containing the Tyrant | **Wesker's betrayal** cutscene, **Tyrant first boss fight** | Tyrant T-002 (boss) |
| **Self-Destruct Terminal** | Computer room with countdown activation | Activate self-destruct sequence | None |
| **Emergency Elevator** | Elevator leading to mansion roof | Escape route during self-destruct | None |
| **Rooftop Helipad** | Open rooftop area with helicopter landing pad | **FINAL BOSS: Tyrant**, Brad's helicopter rescue, Rocket Launcher drop | Tyrant T-002 (final boss) |

## Safe Rooms

Special rooms where the player is completely safe — no enemies can enter. They feature:
- **Typewriter** for saving (requires Ink Ribbons)
- **Item Box** for inventory management
- **Calming music** (contrast to tense ambient sounds)
- Warm, inviting lighting compared to dark corridors

### Safe Room Locations
1. Main Hall (1F)
2. Save Room near Medical Supply Room (1F)
3. Save Room (2F east wing)
4. Guardhouse Save Room
5. Underground Lab Save Room

| Area | Atmosphere | Lighting | Music/Sound | Fear Level |
|------|-----------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Mansion 1F | Gothic elegance decaying | Warm candlelight, moonlight | Grandfather clocks ticking, creaks | Medium |
| Mansion 2F | Darker, more neglected | Dimmer, shadows | Wind, rattling, distant moans | Medium-High |
| Courtyard | Exposed, vulnerable | Moonlight, fog | Insects, wind, dog howls | Medium |
| Guardhouse | Claustrophobic, water-damaged | Flickering fluorescent | Dripping water, plant creaking | High |
| Caves | Primal, trapped | Very dim / torch-lit | Echoing drips, skittering | High |
| Laboratory | Sterile turned chaotic | Harsh fluorescent, some broken | Alarms, machinery hum, footsteps | Very High |
| Rooftop | Open, urgent | Dawn light (reddish) | Helicopter, wind, Tyrant roars | Maximum tension |

## Side-Scroller Level Design Notes

For a side-scroller adaptation, the environments could be organized as sequential levels:

1. **Level 1: Mansion Ground Floor** — Introduce zombies, dogs-through-windows, shotgun acquisition, Moonlight Sonata puzzle
2. **Level 2: Mansion Upper Floor** — Yawn boss fight, more complex layout, crows
3. **Level 3: Courtyard & Gardens** — Outdoor transition, Cerberus packs, crest gate
4. **Level 4: Guardhouse** — Spider enemies, Neptune water hazard, Plant 42 boss
5. **Level 5: Mansion (Return) — Hunter Invasion** — Same mansion but now with Hunters replacing zombies. Terror spike.
6. **Level 6: Underground Caves** — Black Tiger boss, boulder traps, Enrico's death scene
7. **Level 7: Umbrella Laboratory** — Chimeras, high-tech environment, Wesker betrayal cutscene
8. **Level 8: Tyrant & Escape** — Tyrant boss rush, self-destruct timer, rooftop finale with rocket launcher
