# Resident Evil (1996) — Character Reference

## Playable Characters

### Chris Redfield
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team — Point Man / Marksman
- **Age:** 25
- **Background:** Former U.S. Air Force pilot, discharged after clashing with superiors. Recruited to S.T.A.R.S. by Wesker.
- **Personality:** Brave, headstrong, determined. A natural leader. Less cautious than Jill.
- **Gameplay Differences:**
  - **6 inventory slots** (tighter inventory management)
  - Cannot use the lockpick — needs Small Keys found throughout
  - Must find a lighter separately for puzzles
  - Can take slightly more damage before dying
  - Runs slightly faster
  - Exclusive weapon: **Flamethrower** (for specific areas)
  - Partner character: **Rebecca Chambers** (helps with healing and piano puzzle)
  - Campaign is considered **harder** overall
- **Visual:** Muscular build, short brown hair, green S.T.A.R.S. vest over a simple shirt

### Jill Valentine
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team — Rear Security, B&E (Breaking & Entering) Specialist
- **Age:** 23
- **Background:** Former Delta Force operative (U.S. Army), trained in lock-picking and bomb disposal. Half-Japanese, half-French heritage.
- **Personality:** Intelligent, resourceful, calm under pressure. More cautious and investigative.
- **Gameplay Differences:**
  - **8 inventory slots** (more generous)
  - Starts with a **lockpick** (opens simple locks without keys)
  - Starts with a **Beretta** handgun
  - Exclusive weapon: **Grenade Launcher** (Explosive, Acid, and Flame rounds)
  - Partner character: **Barry Burton** (provides weapons, saves from traps)
  - Campaign is considered **easier** overall
- **Visual:** Blue beret, blue S.T.A.R.S. outfit, shoulder-length brown hair
- **Famous title:** "The Master of Unlocking" (as dubbed by Barry)

## Key Supporting Characters

### Albert Wesker
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team Captain / Secret Umbrella Agent
- **Age:** 38
- **Personality:** Cold, calculating, always composed. Speaks in measured tones. Always wears sunglasses even indoors.
- **Visual:** Blond slicked-back hair, black sunglasses, black combat gear
- **True Allegiance:** Umbrella Corporation — double agent
- **Motivation:**
  - Lured S.T.A.R.S. to the mansion as combat data for Umbrella's B.O.W. (Bio Organic Weapon) program
  - Planned to steal the Tyrant and research data for himself
  - Blackmailed Barry into doing his bidding
- **Fate:** Apparently killed by the Tyrant (impaled), but secretly survives (revealed in RE sequels — he infected himself with a virus prototype that granted him superhuman abilities)
- **Significance:** Becomes the primary recurring villain of the entire Resident Evil franchise

### Barry Burton
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team — Weapons Expert
- **Age:** 38
- **Personality:** Loyal, gruff, fatherly. Loves powerful firearms. Comedic due to earnest but awkward dialogue.
- **Visual:** Large, burly man with reddish-brown hair and a full beard. Carries his custom **Colt Python .357 Magnum**.
- **Appears in:** Jill's campaign primarily
- **Story Role:**
  - Acts as Jill's main support — provides weapons, saves her from traps
  - Secretly being **blackmailed by Wesker** (threats against his wife and two daughters)
  - Can be saved or lost depending on player choices at key moments
  - If saved, helps fight in the final escape
- **Key moments:**
  - Saves Jill from the shotgun ceiling trap ("You were almost a Jill sandwich!")
  - Provides the Colt Python magnum to Jill
  - Can betray Jill at Wesker's command, leading to his potential death
- **Famous Lines:**
  - *"You were almost a Jill sandwich!"*
  - *"It's a weapon. It's really powerful, especially against living things."*
  - *"I have THIS!" (pulls magnum)*
  - *"You, the master of unlocking..."*

### Rebecca Chambers
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Bravo Team — Medic / Chemist
- **Age:** 18 (youngest S.T.A.R.S. member)
- **Personality:** Bright, eager, somewhat nervous but brave. Very intelligent.
- **Visual:** Short brown hair, green S.T.A.R.S. vest, youthful appearance
- **Appears in:** Chris's campaign primarily
- **Story Role:**
  - Found hiding in the mansion, sole Bravo Team survivor in Chris's route
  - Assists Chris with healing (uses first aid to restore health)
  - Plays the Moonlight Sonata on the piano (Chris can't play)
  - Helps create the V-JOLT chemical to weaken Plant 42
  - Can be saved or lost depending on player choices
- **Significance:** Later stars in **Resident Evil 0** (prequel)

### Brad Vickers — "Chickenheart"
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team — Helicopter Pilot
- **Personality:** Cowardly, panicky. Abandons the team at the first sign of danger.
- **Story Role:**
  - Flies away at the start when dogs attack, stranding the team
  - Returns at the end to rescue survivors on the rooftop helipad
  - Drops the **Rocket Launcher** used to kill the Tyrant
- **Significance:** Despite his cowardice, his return saves everyone. Later killed by Nemesis in RE3.

### Enrico Marini
- **Role:** S.T.A.R.S. Bravo Team Captain
- **Personality:** Experienced, determined investigator
- **Story Role:**
  - Found wounded in the underground caves
  - Discovers the identity of the S.T.A.R.S. traitor
  - Shot dead mid-sentence by Wesker before he can reveal the truth
  - His death is a pivotal mystery moment

## Other S.T.A.R.S. Members (Found Dead/Dying)

### Kenneth Sullivan
- **Role:** Bravo Team — Chemist
- **Fate:** Eaten by a zombie. The player discovers him being devoured — this IS the famous "first zombie" scene
- **Significance:** His death is arguably the most iconic moment in the game

### Forest Speyer
- **Role:** Bravo Team — Sniper / Omni-Man
- **Fate:** Found dead on the second-floor balcony, body covered in crow pecks
- **Note:** In the Remake, he reanimates as a zombie

### Richard Aiken
- **Role:** Bravo Team — Communications Expert
- **Fate:** Found poisoned by the Yawn (giant snake). The player can attempt to save him with serum, but he typically dies shortly after or sacrifices himself later
- **Significance:** He gives the player a radio before dying

### Joseph Frost
- **Role:** Alpha Team — Vehicle Specialist
- **Fate:** Killed by Cerberus dogs in the opening forest scene. First on-screen death in the game
- **Significance:** His death triggers the panic and retreat into the mansion

## Non-S.T.A.R.S. Characters (Referenced in Files)

### George Trevor
- **Background:** The architect who designed the Spencer Mansion for Ozwell E. Spencer
- **Fate:** After completing the mansion, Spencer had him imprisoned within its walls to keep its secrets. He died wandering the hidden passages.
- **Significance:** Explains the mansion's bizarre, puzzle-filled architecture — it was designed as a trap

### Lisa Trevor (Remake only — not in 1996 original, but important for lore)
- **Background:** George Trevor's daughter, kidnapped by Umbrella and subjected to virus experiments for decades
- **Fate:** Became an unkillable, tragic monster

### Ozwell E. Spencer
- **Background:** Co-founder of Umbrella Corporation, commissioned the Spencer Mansion
- **Significance:** The shadowy figure behind everything. Never appears directly in RE1 but is the reason the mansion and all its horrors exist
